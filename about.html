Welcome to Kongu Nadu Restarunt

Kongu Nadu, a culinary collaboration between Madhampatty Food Services USA and Siruvani Foods. With deep roots in Southern India, Madhampatty Food Services is renowned for its hospitality, authentic catering, and culinary innovation, dedicated to bringing the rich, diverse flavors of Tamil Nadu to the world. We're on a mission to uncover the hidden treasures of this region's cuisine and share its unique heritage with you.

Where Legacy Meets Flavor

Madhampatty Food Services is a leading Food and Beverage services company, founded in Southern part of India and known for their nativity, hospitality, catering services, R&D to bring native flavors of food menu and recipes.

Cuisines from Tamil Nadu in South India has a rich history and unique flavors each inspired by the micro regions of origin. While the cuisine is available globally, the diversity and tradition of the cuisine is not fully explored. Kongu Nadu LLC intends to launch multiple brands and restaurant outlets of the cuisine in USA , each inspired by unique concepts. The first restaurants will be launched in San Jose, California in 2023.
