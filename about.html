Welcome to Kongunaadu by Madhampatty

Authentic Indian Flavours. Rooted in Legacy.

At Kongunadu, every dish is a celebration of bold, rustic flavours from the Kongu region - brought to life with time-honored recipes and heartfelt cooking. From the first spice to the final garnish, our food is crafted with tradition, care, and deep cultural pride.

The Chef Behind the Legacy

Madhampatty T.P. Ranghaararaj is more than a chef - he's a storyteller of taste, culture, and tradition. As the Executive Chef and visionary behind Kongunadu by Madhampatty, he carries forward a rich culinary heritage over 35 years in the making.

Inspired by his father, the legendary Madhampatty Thangavelu, Ranghaararaj masterfully bridges the past and present - preserving the soul of Kongu cuisine while adding his own modern touch. His dishes are rooted in Coimbatore's essence, blending authenticity with creativity, always served with purpose and pride.

From our kitchen to your table, every plate is a tribute - to legacy, to love for food, and to the region that inspires it all.

Our goal

Mission

To celebrate the bold, authentic flavors of Kongu Nadu by bringing its rich culinary heritage to every dish we serve. We're committed to delivering high-quality, nutritious, and hygienic South Indian cuisine - all year round. With a focus on tradition, taste, and balance, we strive to offer a dining experience that's truly one-of-a-kind.

Vision

To lead a culinary movement that redefines South Indian cuisine for a global audience - blending timeless traditions with fresh, modern innovation. By honoring the distinct essence of Kovai and the broader Kongu region, we aim to craft experiences that delight the senses, exceed expectations, and position us as pioneers in the evolving world of Indian dining.
